If you have had deep fried prawns with the shell on, you will relate to me when I say that the fried crabs taste simply yummy.
Simple to make and serve as a snack/appetiser or as a side when eating a xitt-kodi (Goan for rice & curry).
Serves: 2 to 4 Persons Prep. Time: 10 Mins. + marination Cooking Time: 20 Mins. Ingredients:

  1. Sea Crabs (cleaned) – 4 big sized (about 600 gms with the shell)
  2. For the Marinade:
  3. Red Chilly Powder – 1 tbsp.
  4. Turmeric Powder – ½ tsp.
  5. Black Pepper (coarsely ground) – ½ tsp.
  6. Garam Masala Powder – ¼ tsp.
  7. Juice of 1 big lime.
  8. Salt – ¼ tsp. or to taste
  9. Wheat Flour – as needed
  10. Oil – 3 to 4 tbsp.
